Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10th April 2026

This app respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or contact details.

We may collect anonymous usage data through third-party services to improve the app experience.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This may include information such as device type, app usage, session duration, and general location (country-level only). This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Purchases

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat processes purchase-related data such as transaction status and subscription information (if applicable). This data is used only to provide access to purchased content and is not used for identification outside the app.

Advertising

The app may display ads. Ad providers may use limited data to show relevant ads. We do not directly control how third-party ad networks use data.

Data Usage

The data collected is used to:

  • Improve app performance
  • Fix issues and bugs
  • Understand feature usage
  • Manage purchases

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Data is only shared with trusted third-party providers (Firebase and RevenueCat) to operate the app.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data, but no system is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app means you accept any updates.
